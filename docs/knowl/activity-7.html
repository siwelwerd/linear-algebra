Activity 1.2.4.

Recall that a matrix is in reduced row echelon form (RREF) if

  1. The leading term (first nonzero term) of each nonzero row is a 1. Call these terms pivots.
  2. Each pivot is to the right of every higher pivot.
  3. Each term above or below a pivot is zero.
  4. All rows of zeroes are at the bottom of the matrix.
  1. \begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 2 & 4 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \end{equation*}
  3. \begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & 2 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \end{array}\right] \end{equation*}
  4. \begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 0 & 3 & 3 & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \end{equation*}
  5. \begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 0 & 1 & 0 & 7 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 & 4 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right] \end{equation*}
  6. \begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 4 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \end{array}\right] \end{equation*}

For each matrix, circle the leading terms, and label it as RREF or not RREF. For the ones not in RREF, find their RREF.
